## Images
Store images inside your content tree and reference them by path:

```md
![Diagram](./images/image1.png)
```

## VeslxGallery
Use the built-in VeslxGallery component for image grids:

```mdx
<VeslxGallery
  path="images"
  globs={["image1.png", "image2.png"]}
  baseUrl="https://cdn.example.com/assets"
  title="Sample images"
  caption="A small example gallery."
  captionLabel="Figure 1"
/>

When `baseUrl` is set, VeslxGallery still uses your local content tree for globbing but loads images from the CDN URL.

### Full-width scroll row
Galleries with more than three images become a scrollable row that spans the screen.

```mdx
<VeslxGallery
  path="images"
  globs={["image1.png", "image2.png", "image3.png", "image4.png", "image5.png", "image6.png", "image7.png", "image8.png", "image9.png", "image1.png"]}
  title="More images"
  caption="A wider gallery row with more than three images."
  captionLabel="Figure 2"
/>
